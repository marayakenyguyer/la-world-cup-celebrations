About the Program

The World Cup
in Every Neighborhood

While FIFA fan fests anchor downtown LA, Mayor Karen Bass's Community Celebrations program ensures the World Cup reaches every corner of the city — from the San Fernando Valley to the Harbor, from Westside parks to East LA recreation centers.

These free, family-friendly events transform City of LA Recreation & Parks sites into vibrant gathering spaces where Angelenos watch the matches on large LED screens surrounded by youth soccer clinics, neighborhood programming, and community resources. No ticket, no cost, no barriers.

🗺️
Citywide Reach

Sites span all 15 Council Districts, from Sylmar and Northridge to Wilmington and Venice Beach.

🔄
Rotating Schedule

3 sites activate each match day, with every site hosting 7 celebrations across the tournament.

🌟
A Legacy Investment

This program pilots the volunteer infrastructure and activation model that will scale to the 2027 Super Bowl and 2028 Olympic & Paralympic Games.

June 12 – July 19

Tournament dates for the 2026 FIFA World Cup, with the Final played right here in Los Angeles.

All 15 Districts

Every Council District will host celebrations, ensuring no neighborhood is left out of the World Cup experience.

Free for All

No tickets, no registration required. Just show up, bring your blankets and chairs, and enjoy the game with your neighbors.

What's at Every Celebration

What Residents Can Expect

Every Community Celebration includes a core set of free activities. Select sites may offer additional programming based on their neighborhood and Council District.

📺

Live Match Screenings

Large mobile LED screens broadcast every World Cup match in real-time so you don't miss a goal.

⚽

PlayLA Soccer Clinics

Free youth soccer mini-clinics and skill activities run by the City's PlayLA program for kids of all ages.

🎉

Family Fun Zones

Interactive activities, flag-making stations, and family-friendly programming for all ages.

🏛️

City Services & Resources

Resource tables connecting residents with City departments, community organizations, and local services.

🎵

Music & Arts

Select sites feature live music, cultural performances, dance, and visual arts activations that reflect neighborhood identity.

🍜

Local Vendors & Culture

Some celebrations include local food vendors, small businesses, and neighborhood-led cultural activations.
